Metaphor Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Metaphor ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our tarot card reading application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

Metaphor is designed with privacy in mind. The App does not require user registration and does not directly collect personal information.

When you use Metaphor:

  • We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do not store your reading history on our servers

3. AI-Powered Features

Metaphor uses third-party AI services to generate tarot card interpretations. When you request a reading:

  • Your questions or prompts are sent to external AI services for processing
  • We do not store your questions or AI responses on our own servers
  • The AI service provider may process this data according to their own privacy policy
Important: Please avoid entering sensitive personal information (such as full names, addresses, financial details, or health information) when using the tarot reading feature.

4. In-App Purchases

Metaphor offers consumable in-app purchases. All payment transactions are processed entirely by:

  • Apple App Store (for iOS devices)
  • Google Play Store (for Android devices)

We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. All purchase-related data is handled according to Apple's or Google's respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Third-Party Services

Metaphor integrates with the following third-party services solely for processing in-app purchases:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Handles all payment processing for in-app purchases

These platforms may collect certain information as part of their standard operations. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more details.

6. Children's Privacy

Metaphor is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk of data breach from our end. Your reading data remains on your device and under your control.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete the App and all associated local data at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns
  • Request information about how your data is handled

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
